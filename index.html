Summary of the History of Personal Computer

  1. The first personal computer was the Altair 8800 invented by Ed Roberts in 1975
  2. Apple II, launched in 1977 was the first computer to store its operating system in ROM
  3. VisiCalc was the first spreadsheet application. It was invented by Dan Bricklin and Bob Frankston in 1979
  4. Xerox PARC made Alto, the first PC with a GUI
  5. The microprocessor chip was invented by Intel
  6. Gary Killdal made CP/M, the first PC operating system
  7. Tim Patterson made QDOS, an OS similar to CP/M which was later bought by Microsoft and licensed to IBM
  8. Lotus 1-2-3 was the spreadsheet used in IBM's first PC
  9. IBM's first PC was launched in 1981
  10. John Warnock co-founded Adobe. He also invented a technology that allowed the laser printer to print exactly what is on the screen, commonly known as WYSIWIG - what you see is what you get
  11. Nestcape was the first computer browser. It was strangled out of the market when Microsoft made Internet Explorer and provided it for free on every Windows computer beginning 1995.
  12. Personal computers have become smaller and faster since 1975 due to advancement in technology
