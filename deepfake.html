Summary of Deep Fakes

  1. Deep Fakes refers to realistic photo, audio, video and other forgeries created using Artificial Intelligence (AI)

    2. Origin of Deepfakes

    1. The term deepfake originated in 2017, when an anonymous Reddit user called himself “Deepfakes.” This Reddit user manipulated Google's open-source, deep-learning technology to create and post manipulated pornographic videos. The videos were doctored with a technique known as face-swapping. The user “Deepfakes” replaced real faces with celebrity faces.

    Uses of Deep Fakes

  2. Deep Fakes can be used for various purposes, the most common one being for entertainment.
  3. In entertainment, the technology is used for special effecrs and animations.
    4. However, they could aso be used for fraudulent acitvities.
  4. The technology can create people who don't exist and it can show real people apepearing to say and do things they did not actually say or do.
  5. Some nefarious use of deep fakes include; scams and hoaxes, public manipulation, social engineering and identity fraud.

How to spot a deep fake

  1. Unnatural eye movement
  2. Bad lip-syncing
  3. Digital background noise
  4. Robotic-sounding voices
  5. Awkward head and body positioning
    There is technology being developed to identify deep fakes. This will mitigate the impact of Deep Fakes.
